FAQ

Questions we
hear often.

How is this different from using a website builder like Webflow or Squarespace?
Website builders give you tools. We give you a finished, conversion-optimised website. Our AI doesn't use templates — it designs from scratch based on your specific brand, audience, and goals. And unlike any builder, it continues optimising your site after it goes live.
Do I own the website and code after it's built?
Yes, completely. We transfer full ownership of the codebase, domain, and hosting setup to you on launch day. There are no ongoing licensing fees or lock-in. The continuous optimisation feature is optional — it runs as an add-on service, not a requirement.
What does "AI-generated design" actually mean?
Our AI has been trained on 50,000+ high-performing commercial websites. Given your brief, it generates unique layouts, typography hierarchies, spacing systems, and component libraries — all from scratch. A senior designer then reviews and refines the output before it reaches you. It's not a template. It's not a tool. It's a completely original design created for your brand.
Five days sounds too fast. What's the catch?
There isn't one. The speed comes from eliminating the idle time that slows traditional agencies — waiting on designers, developers, handoffs, and feedback loops. AI compresses all of that. The 5-day timeline applies to projects up to 12 pages. Larger enterprise builds run on a custom schedule, but still significantly faster than the industry norm.
What if I don't like the first designs?
Growth and Enterprise clients get unlimited revision rounds. Starter clients get two. In practice, over 90% of clients approve designs after the first or second round. If you have a clear brief, the AI produces results that are directionally right from the start — refinement is usually about details, not direction.
How does continuous optimisation work after launch?
Our AI monitors user behaviour on your live site — click patterns, scroll depth, drop-off points, conversion funnels. It identifies friction points and automatically generates and tests micro-improvements: button placement, copy changes, layout adjustments. You receive a monthly report of changes made and results achieved. No manual input required.
