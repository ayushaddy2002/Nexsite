FAQ

Questions we
hear often.

What services does Nexsite offer?
We offer Android and iOS app development, website development, and Figma prototyping. Every project comes with free lifetime maintenance included — no extra charge, ever.
Do I own the code after the project is built?
Yes, completely. Full ownership of the codebase, design files, and all project assets is transferred to you on delivery. There are no licensing fees, no lock-in, and no ongoing costs unless you choose our extended support plans.
What does free lifetime maintenance actually mean?
Any bug fixes, minor updates, or content changes are handled by our team at no extra cost — forever. We are the first software company to offer this. It's our commitment that the relationship doesn't end at delivery.
How long does a project take?
A Figma prototype is ready within 3 days. The full build timeline depends on scope and complexity, but we always aim for the shortest delivery time possible without compromising quality.
How much does a project cost?
No project, regardless of complexity, will exceed a 5-digit budget. We keep costs transparent and affordable from the first conversation. Book a free call to get a tailored quote for your specific project.
Do you offer in-person meetings?
Yes. We offer in-person meetings for clients who prefer face-to-face discussions. Contact us to schedule a meeting at a time and location that works for you. We believe real relationships build better products.
